Our Story

About Minaret Monarch

Founded on faith, built on community, guided by knowledge.

Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings
to a Thriving Centre

Minaret Monarch began over 15 years ago as a small gathering of dedicated Muslims who dreamed of a place where faith could flourish. What started in a community hall has grown into a landmark Islamic centre serving thousands of families.

Our founders believed a mosque should be more than a place of prayer — it should be a hub of learning, compassion, and service. That vision has guided every decision, from the architecture of our building to the breadth of our programs.

Today, Minaret Monarch stands as a beacon of Islamic excellence — recognised for its scholarship, warmth, and commitment to serving the Muslim community and society at large.


Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To establish a premier Islamic centre serving as a spiritual home, educational institution, and community resource for Muslims of all backgrounds.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and impactful Islamic centre in the region — nurturing generations of confident, knowledgeable, and compassionate Muslims.

Our Values

Taqwa, Ilm, Rahma, Adl, and Shura — God-consciousness, knowledge, compassion, justice, and consultation guide everything we do.
